Every reported issue comes with a tracking system that provides updates from submission to resolution, keeping citizens informed throughout the process.

Citizens can support issues reported by others, comment with additional information, and collaborate to address community challenges.

City officials and administrators have dedicated dashboards to manage, respond to, and resolve issues efficiently with direct citizen communication.
